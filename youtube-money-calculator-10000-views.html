How Much Does YouTube Pay for 10,000 Views?

Most monetized YouTube creators earn between $10 and $300 for 10,000 views, depending on CPM, RPM, audience location and niche.

Channels focused on finance, investing, software and business often earn significantly more than entertainment channels.

Quick Answer
