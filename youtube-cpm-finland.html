YouTube CPM Denmark (2026)

Denmark is one of the strongest advertising markets in Northern Europe, with high digital adoption and premium advertisers.

Creators targeting Danish viewers often benefit from CPM rates well above the global average.

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in Denmark?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from Danish audiences can often earn between $4,500 and $11,000.

Channels in finance, software and business niches can earn significantly more.

Why Is Denmark CPM High?

  • Strong economy
  • High consumer spending
  • Premium Nordic advertisers
  • Excellent ecommerce market
  • Strong ad conversion rates
