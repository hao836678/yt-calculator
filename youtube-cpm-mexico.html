YouTube CPM Mexico (2026)

Mexico is one of the largest YouTube markets in the Spanish-speaking world. Millions of users watch YouTube every day across entertainment, gaming, education and finance categories.

Although CPM rates are lower than the United States and Western Europe, Mexico offers massive audience reach and excellent growth potential for creators.

Average YouTube CPM in Mexico

Metric Value
Average CPM $0.80 - $3.50
Finance CPM $4 - $12+
Business CPM $3 - $10+
Technology CPM $2 - $8+
Entertainment CPM $0.50 - $2

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in Mexico?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from Mexican audiences can often earn between $800 and $3,500.

Channels focused on finance, technology and business content generally earn significantly more than entertainment channels.

Why Is Mexico CPM Lower Than the USA?

  • Lower advertiser budgets
  • Lower average purchasing power
  • Less advertiser competition
  • Large entertainment-focused audience
  • Developing digital advertising market

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mexico good for YouTube growth?

Yes. Mexico is one of the largest Spanish-speaking YouTube markets and offers excellent audience growth opportunities.

How much can 100,000 views earn in Mexico?

Many creators earn between $80 and $350 depending on niche and audience quality.

Which niche has the highest CPM in Mexico?

Finance, investing, software, technology and business content generally achieve the highest CPM rates.
