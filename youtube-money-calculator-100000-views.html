How Much Does YouTube Pay for 100,000 Views?

Reaching 100,000 YouTube views is a major milestone for many creators. Depending on CPM, RPM, audience location and content category, creators can earn anywhere from $100 to $3,000 or more.

Channels focused on finance, investing, software, AI and business generally receive higher advertising rates than entertainment content.

Quick Answer

Most monetized YouTube channels earn between $100 and $3,000 for 100,000 views. Some premium niches can exceed these estimates.

Can 100,000 Views Make a Full-Time Income?

For most creators, 100,000 views on a single video will not create a full-time income by itself. However, consistent uploads generating hundreds of thousands of monthly views can become a meaningful revenue source.

Many successful creators combine YouTube ad revenue with affiliate marketing, sponsorships, digital products and memberships.
