YouTube CPM India (2026)

India is one of the largest YouTube markets in the world. Millions of creators publish content for Indian audiences every day.

Although CPM rates in India are lower than countries like the United States and Australia, large audiences can still generate significant revenue.

Average YouTube CPM in India

Metric Value
Average CPM $0.30 - $2.00
Finance CPM $2 - $8
Business CPM $1 - $5
Technology CPM $1 - $4
Entertainment CPM $0.20 - $1.50

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in India?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from India may earn between $300 and $2,000.

Channels operating in finance, software and business niches can earn significantly more than entertainment channels.

Why Is India CPM Lower?

  • Lower advertiser spending compared to Western countries
  • Lower average consumer purchasing power
  • Higher creator competition
  • Large volume of inventory available to advertisers
  • Lower average cost per customer acquisition

Frequently Asked Questions

Is India a low CPM country?

Yes. India generally has lower CPM rates than the United States, Canada and Australia.

How much can 100,000 views earn in India?

Many creators earn between $30 and $200 depending on niche and audience quality.

Can Indian channels make good money?

Yes. Large audiences and strong subscriber growth can generate substantial income even with lower CPM rates.
