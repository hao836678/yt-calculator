How to Choose a Channel Idea

  1. Choose a topic you enjoy.
  2. Research audience demand.
  3. Check monetization potential.
  4. Create 20–30 content ideas before launching.
  5. Stay consistent for at least 6 months.

What is the best YouTube channel idea in 2026?

AI tools, personal finance and software tutorials are among the strongest opportunities for new creators.

What niche has the highest CPM?

Finance, investing and insurance consistently rank among the highest-paying YouTube niches.

Can beginners start a faceless YouTube channel?

Yes. Many successful creators build channels using screen recordings, tutorials, animations and voiceovers.
