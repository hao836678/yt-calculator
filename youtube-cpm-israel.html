YouTube CPM Israel (2026)

Israel is one of the highest CPM markets in the Middle East, driven by strong technology, SaaS, finance and startup ecosystems.

Creators targeting Israeli audiences often benefit from premium advertiser spending and high conversion rates.

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in Israel?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from Israeli audiences can often earn between $5,000 and $14,000.

Finance, SaaS, AI and startup-related channels may earn substantially more.

Why Is Israel CPM High?

  • Strong tech economy
  • Premium SaaS advertisers
  • High purchasing power
  • Strong startup ecosystem
  • High conversion traffic
