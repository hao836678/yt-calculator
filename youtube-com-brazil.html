YouTube CPM Brazil (2026)

Brazil has one of the largest YouTube audiences in the world. Millions of viewers watch content daily across entertainment, gaming, finance and education categories.

While CPM rates are lower than the United States and Western Europe, Brazil offers enormous traffic potential and strong growth opportunities.

Average YouTube CPM in Brazil

Metric Value
Average CPM $0.80 - $3
Finance CPM $4 - $12+
Business CPM $3 - $10+
Technology CPM $2 - $8+
Entertainment CPM $0.50 - $2

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in Brazil?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from Brazilian audiences can often earn between $800 and $3,000.

Channels in finance, investing and technology typically earn more than entertainment channels.

Why Is Brazil CPM Lower?

  • Lower advertiser budgets
  • Lower average consumer spending
  • Less competition among advertisers
  • Large entertainment-focused audience
  • Emerging advertising market

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Brazil good for YouTube growth?

Yes. Brazil is one of the largest YouTube markets worldwide and offers excellent opportunities for audience growth.

How much can 100,000 views earn in Brazil?

Most creators earn between $80 and $300 depending on niche and audience quality.

Which niche has the highest CPM in Brazil?

Finance, investing, cryptocurrency, software and business content generally receive the highest CPM rates.
