YouTube CPM Ireland (2026)

Ireland is one of Europe’s premium digital advertising markets, supported by strong tech investment and high consumer spending.

Creators targeting Irish audiences often receive CPM rates significantly above the global average.

Average YouTube CPM in Ireland

Metric Value
Average CPM$4 - $11
Finance CPM$12 - $30+
Business CPM$8 - $24+
Technology CPM$6 - $20+
Entertainment CPM$2 - $6

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in Ireland?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from Irish audiences can often earn between $4,000 and $11,000.

Finance, SaaS, software and business channels usually earn much more.

Why Is Ireland CPM High?

  • Strong economy
  • Major tech companies headquartered in Ireland
  • High purchasing power
  • Premium advertisers
  • Strong digital ad conversion
