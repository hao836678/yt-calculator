How Much Does YouTube Pay for 5,000,000 Views?

Reaching 5 million YouTube views is a major milestone that can generate substantial income for creators.

Actual earnings depend on CPM, RPM, audience country, viewer engagement and content niche.

Quick Answer

Most monetized YouTube channels earn between $5,000 and $150,000 for 5 million views.
