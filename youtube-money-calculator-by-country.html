YouTube Earnings by Country (2026)

YouTube earnings vary significantly depending on audience location. Advertisers pay much higher CPM rates in countries such as the United States, Australia, Canada and the United Kingdom compared to developing markets.

This guide compares estimated YouTube revenue by country and shows how much creators can earn from 1 million views.

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views?

A channel receiving 1 million views from a United States audience may generate several times more revenue than a channel receiving the same number of views from lower CPM countries. Audience quality is often more important than total view count.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which country has the highest YouTube CPM?

Australia and the United States consistently rank among the highest CPM countries.

Does YouTube pay differently by country?

Yes. Advertiser demand and purchasing power strongly influence CPM rates.

Can low CPM countries still make money?

Yes. Large audiences can still generate substantial revenue despite lower CPM rates.
