YouTube CPM UK (2026)

The United Kingdom is one of the highest-paying YouTube markets in Europe. UK advertisers invest heavily in digital marketing, making CPM rates attractive for creators.

Channels targeting British audiences often earn significantly more than channels focused on developing markets.

Average YouTube CPM in the UK

Metric Value
Average CPM $4 - $11
Finance CPM $12 - $35+
Business CPM $8 - $25+
Technology CPM $6 - $18+
Entertainment CPM $2 - $7

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in the UK?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from UK audiences may earn between $4,000 and $11,000.

Finance, investing, software and business content generally achieve the highest earnings.

Why Is UK CPM High?

  • Strong advertiser demand
  • High consumer spending power
  • Competitive finance sector
  • Large software and SaaS market
  • Premium English-speaking audience

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the UK a high CPM country?

Yes. The UK consistently ranks among the highest CPM countries worldwide.

How much can 100,000 views earn in the UK?

Many creators earn between $400 and $1,100 depending on niche and audience quality.

Is UK CPM higher than India?

Yes. UK CPM rates are often several times higher than India's average CPM.
