YouTube CPM AI (2026)

Artificial Intelligence is one of the fastest-growing content categories on YouTube. AI tools, automation software and business applications attract premium advertisers.

Because many AI products operate on subscription models, companies are willing to pay higher CPM rates to acquire customers.

Average AI Channel CPM

Audience Region Typical CPM
United States $8 - $30+
Canada $6 - $25+
United Kingdom $7 - $28+
Australia $8 - $30+
India $1 - $8

How Much Can an AI Channel Earn?

A successful AI-focused channel can earn thousands of dollars from every million views, especially when targeting audiences in Tier 1 countries.

Many AI creators also generate affiliate commissions from software subscriptions, increasing total revenue significantly.

Why AI CPM Is High

  • High-value SaaS products
  • Subscription business models
  • Business-focused audiences
  • Rapid industry growth
  • Strong advertiser competition

Frequently Asked Questions

Is AI a high CPM niche?

Yes. AI is currently one of the highest CPM categories on YouTube.

Can AI channels earn affiliate income?

Absolutely. Many AI creators earn more through affiliate software programs than ad revenue.

Which AI topics pay the most?

AI tools, automation, ChatGPT, SaaS reviews and business AI applications usually attract the highest CPM rates.
