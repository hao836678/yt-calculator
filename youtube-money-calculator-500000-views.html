How Much Does YouTube Pay for 500,000 Views?

Reaching 500,000 YouTube views is a significant milestone for creators. Depending on CPM, RPM, audience country and niche, earnings can vary dramatically.

Channels in finance, investing, software, business and AI often earn far more than entertainment channels because advertisers pay higher CPM rates.

Quick Answer

Most monetized YouTube channels earn between $500 and $15,000 for 500,000 views.

Can 500,000 Views Make a Full-Time Income?

For creators in high CPM niches, 500,000 views can generate meaningful income. Many creators combine ad revenue with affiliate marketing, sponsorships and digital products for even higher earnings.
