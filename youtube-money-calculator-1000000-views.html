How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1,000,000 Views?

Reaching 1 million YouTube views is a major milestone for content creators. Depending on CPM, RPM, audience country and content niche, creators can earn anywhere from $1,000 to more than $30,000.

Channels focused on finance, investing, artificial intelligence, software tutorials and business education generally receive the highest advertising rates.

Quick Answer

Most monetized YouTube channels earn between $1,000 and $30,000 for 1 million views. Premium niches can exceed these estimates significantly.

Can 1 Million Views Make a Full-Time Income?

Yes. For many creators, a video reaching one million views can generate meaningful income. In high-CPM niches, a single video can earn several thousand dollars or more.

Many successful creators also increase revenue through sponsorships, affiliate marketing, digital products, memberships and consulting services.
