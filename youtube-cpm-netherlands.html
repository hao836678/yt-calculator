YouTube CPM Netherlands (2026)

The Netherlands is one of Europe's strongest digital advertising markets. Dutch advertisers invest heavily in finance, software, ecommerce and business services.

Because of high purchasing power and advanced online commerce, Dutch YouTube audiences typically generate higher CPM rates than the global average.

Average YouTube CPM in the Netherlands

Metric Value
Average CPM $4 - $10
Finance CPM $12 - $30+
Business CPM $8 - $25+
Technology CPM $6 - $20+
Entertainment CPM $2 - $6

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in the Netherlands?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from Dutch audiences can often earn between $4,000 and $10,000.

Finance, software and business-focused channels may earn significantly more due to premium advertisers.

Why Is Netherlands CPM So High?

  • Strong digital economy
  • High consumer spending
  • Advanced ecommerce market
  • Premium finance advertisers
  • Competitive SaaS and software industry

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Netherlands a high CPM country?

Yes. The Netherlands consistently ranks among the highest CPM countries in Europe due to strong advertiser demand.

How much can 100,000 views earn in the Netherlands?

Many creators earn between $400 and $1,000 depending on audience quality and content niche.

Which niche has the highest CPM in the Netherlands?

Finance, investing, business software, insurance and technology channels generally achieve the highest CPM rates.
