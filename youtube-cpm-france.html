YouTube CPM France (2026)

France is one of the strongest YouTube advertising markets in Europe. French advertisers spend heavily on finance, business, software, luxury products and ecommerce campaigns.

As a result, channels targeting French audiences often earn significantly higher CPM rates than creators targeting developing countries.

Average YouTube CPM in France

Metric Value
Average CPM $3 - $8
Finance CPM $10 - $30+
Business CPM $8 - $25+
Technology CPM $6 - $20+
Entertainment CPM $2 - $6

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in France?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from French audiences can often earn between $3,000 and $8,000.

Channels focused on finance, investing, business and software may earn substantially more than entertainment channels.

Why Is France CPM High?

  • Strong European advertising market
  • High consumer purchasing power
  • Premium finance advertisers
  • Growing software and SaaS sector
  • Strong ecommerce competition

Frequently Asked Questions

Is France a good country for YouTube CPM?

Yes. France consistently ranks among the higher CPM countries in Europe and often outperforms many international markets.

How much can 100,000 views earn in France?

Many creators earn between $300 and $800 depending on audience quality and content niche.

Which niche has the highest CPM in France?

Finance, investing, business software, insurance and technology channels generally receive the highest CPM rates.
