What Is YouTube CPM?

CPM stands for Cost Per Mille, meaning the amount advertisers pay for one thousand ad impressions. Higher CPM rates generally indicate more valuable audiences and stronger advertiser demand.

How to Increase YouTube CPM

  • Target audiences in high CPM countries.
  • Create content in finance, software and business niches.
  • Increase watch time and viewer retention.
  • Produce advertiser-friendly content.
  • Build authority in valuable topics.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good YouTube CPM?

Many creators consider CPMs above $5 to be strong, although results vary by niche and audience location.

Which country has the highest CPM?

The United States, Australia, Canada and the United Kingdom generally have some of the highest CPM rates.

Which niche has the highest CPM?

Finance, investing, insurance and software channels often receive the highest CPM rates.
