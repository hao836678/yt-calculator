YouTube CPM Japan (2026)

Japan is one of the largest digital advertising markets in the world and remains a valuable country for YouTube creators.

Japanese audiences have strong purchasing power and attract premium advertisers from technology, finance and consumer brands.

Average YouTube CPM in Japan

Metric Value
Average CPM $3 - $8
Finance CPM $8 - $25+
Business CPM $6 - $18+
Technology CPM $5 - $15+
Entertainment CPM $2 - $5

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in Japan?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from Japanese audiences may earn between $3,000 and $8,000.

Technology, finance and business channels generally achieve the highest CPM rates.

Why Is Japan CPM Strong?

  • Large digital advertising market
  • Strong consumer purchasing power
  • Premium technology advertisers
  • Established e-commerce ecosystem
  • High engagement audiences

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Japan a high CPM country?

Yes. Japan is one of the strongest CPM markets in Asia.

How much can 100,000 views earn in Japan?

Many creators earn between $300 and $800 depending on niche and audience quality.

Which niche performs best in Japan?

Technology, finance, investing and business content often generate the highest CPM rates.
