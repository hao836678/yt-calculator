YouTube CPM South Korea (2026)

South Korea is one of Asia's most advanced digital economies. Korean advertisers spend heavily on technology, ecommerce, finance, gaming and consumer electronics.

Because of strong purchasing power and a highly connected population, South Korea offers some of the highest CPM rates in Asia.

Average YouTube CPM in South Korea

Metric Value
Average CPM $3 - $9
Finance CPM $10 - $25+
Business CPM $8 - $20+
Technology CPM $8 - $22+
Entertainment CPM $2 - $5

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in South Korea?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from South Korean audiences can often earn between $3,000 and $9,000.

Technology, finance and gaming channels frequently earn above-average CPM rates because advertisers compete aggressively for Korean consumers.

Why Is South Korea CPM So High?

  • High internet penetration
  • Strong ecommerce market
  • Premium technology advertisers
  • Competitive gaming industry
  • High consumer spending power

Frequently Asked Questions

Is South Korea a high CPM country?

Yes. South Korea is generally one of the highest CPM countries in Asia and often outperforms many global markets.

How much can 100,000 views earn in South Korea?

Many creators earn between $300 and $900 depending on audience quality and niche.

Which niche has the highest CPM in South Korea?

Technology, finance, software, investing and gaming content generally achieve the highest CPM rates.
