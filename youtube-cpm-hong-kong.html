YouTube CPM Hong Kong (2026)

Hong Kong is one of Asia’s highest-paying advertising markets, driven by strong finance, luxury and technology sectors.

Creators targeting Hong Kong audiences often enjoy CPM rates significantly above global averages.

Average YouTube CPM in Hong Kong

Metric Value
Average CPM$6 - $16
Finance CPM$20 - $50+

Technology CPM$10 - $28+

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in Hong Kong?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from Hong Kong audiences can often earn between $6,000 and $16,000.

Finance, stock market, SaaS and luxury channels may earn substantially more.

Why Is Hong Kong CPM High?

  • Global finance hub
  • Luxury advertisers
  • High purchasing power
  • Premium technology advertisers
  • Strong advertiser competition
