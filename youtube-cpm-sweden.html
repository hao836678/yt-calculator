YouTube CPM Sweden (2026)

Sweden is one of Northern Europe's strongest advertising markets, offering relatively high CPM rates for YouTube creators.

Channels targeting Swedish audiences often earn above global averages thanks to strong purchasing power and premium advertisers.

Average YouTube CPM in Sweden

Metric Value
Average CPM $4 - $10
Finance CPM $12 - $30+
Business CPM $8 - $22+
Technology CPM $6 - $18+
Entertainment CPM $2 - $6

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in Sweden?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from Swedish audiences can often earn between $4,000 and $10,000.

High-value niches such as finance, SaaS and investing may generate significantly more.

Why Is Sweden CPM High?

  • Strong economy
  • High online purchasing power
  • Premium Nordic advertisers
  • Excellent digital adoption
  • Strong conversion rates

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Sweden a high CPM country?

Yes. Sweden ranks among the higher CPM countries in Europe.

How much can 100,000 views earn in Sweden?

Many creators earn between $400 and $1,000 depending on niche.

Finance, software, investing and business content usually perform best.
