YouTube CPM Finance (2026)

Finance is widely considered one of the highest-paying niches on YouTube. Banks, brokers, insurance companies and fintech platforms compete heavily for advertising space.

As a result, finance channels often achieve CPM rates several times higher than entertainment or gaming channels.

Average Finance Channel CPM

Audience Region Typical CPM
United States $15 - $50+
Canada $10 - $30+
United Kingdom $12 - $35+
Australia $15 - $40+
India $2 - $8

How Much Can a Finance Channel Earn?

A finance channel receiving 1 million views may earn anywhere from $5,000 to over $50,000 depending on audience location and advertiser demand.

Many finance creators also generate additional income through affiliate marketing, sponsorships and premium products.

Why Finance CPM Is So High

  • Financial customers have high lifetime value
  • Banks compete aggressively for leads
  • Insurance advertisers pay premium rates
  • Brokerage platforms have large marketing budgets
  • Credit card and loan companies spend heavily online

Frequently Asked Questions

Is finance the highest CPM niche?

Finance is consistently ranked among the highest CPM niches on YouTube.

How much can 100,000 finance views earn?

Depending on audience location, 100,000 views may generate hundreds or even thousands of dollars.

Which finance topics pay the most?

Investing, credit cards, loans, insurance and personal finance generally attract the highest advertiser bids.
