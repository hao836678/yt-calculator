YouTube CPM UAE (2026)

The United Arab Emirates is one of the highest CPM regions in the Middle East, especially for creators targeting Dubai audiences.

Luxury brands, finance companies, real estate advertisers and premium ecommerce businesses heavily compete for UAE traffic.

Average YouTube CPM in UAE

Metric Value
Average CPM$6 - $18
Finance CPM$20 - $50+
Business CPM$15 - $35+
Real Estate CPM$18 - $45+
Entertainment CPM$3 - $8

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in UAE?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from UAE audiences can often earn between $6,000 and $18,000.

Finance, luxury, crypto and real estate channels may earn significantly more.

Why Is UAE CPM So High?

  • Very high purchasing power
  • Luxury advertisers
  • Premium real estate market
  • High finance and crypto demand
  • Strong advertiser competition

Frequently Asked Questions

Yes. UAE is one of the highest CPM markets in Asia and the Middle East.
