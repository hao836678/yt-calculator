What Is YouTube RPM?

RPM stands for Revenue Per Mille. It represents the actual revenue creators receive for every 1,000 views after YouTube takes its share.

RPM vs CPM

Metric Meaning
CPM Advertiser Cost Per 1,000 Ad Impressions
RPM Creator Revenue Per 1,000 Views

Why RPM Matters

  • Shows actual creator earnings.
  • Measures channel monetization efficiency.
  • Helps compare performance across niches.
  • More useful than CPM for revenue planning.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good YouTube RPM?

Many channels achieve RPMs between $1 and $5, while finance and business channels can exceed $10.

Why is RPM lower than CPM?

RPM reflects creator earnings after YouTube's revenue share and includes all views, not just monetized views.

Can RPM increase over time?

Yes. Better audiences, stronger engagement and higher-value niches often improve RPM.
