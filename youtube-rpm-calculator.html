What Is RPM?

RPM stands for Revenue Per Mille. It represents how much money a creator earns per 1,000 views after YouTube takes its share.

RPM vs CPM

  • CPM = Advertiser cost per 1,000 impressions
  • RPM = Creator revenue per 1,000 views

Average YouTube RPM

  • USA: $2 - $8
  • UK: $1.5 - $6
  • Canada: $1.5 - $5
  • Australia: $2 - $7
  • India: $0.2 - $1

RPM Earnings Examples

  • 100,000 views at $3 RPM = $300
  • 1,000,000 views at $3 RPM = $3,000
  • 10,000,000 views at $3 RPM = $30,000
