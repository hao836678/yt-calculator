YouTube CPM Belgium (2026)

Belgium has a strong European advertising market with high-value audiences and premium advertisers across finance, insurance and technology.

Creators targeting Belgian viewers typically earn CPM rates above the global average.

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in Belgium?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from Belgian audiences can often earn between $3,500 and $9,000.

Finance, SaaS and business channels usually earn substantially more.

Why Is Belgium CPM High?

  • Strong economy
  • Premium EU advertisers
  • High purchasing power
  • Good ad conversion rates
  • Strong digital commerce market
