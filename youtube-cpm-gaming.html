YouTube CPM Gaming (2026)

Gaming is one of the largest categories on YouTube. Millions of viewers watch gameplay videos, livestream highlights, tutorials and gaming news every day.

Although gaming CPM is usually lower than finance and business niches, gaming channels can generate massive traffic volumes.

Average Gaming Channel CPM

Audience Region Typical CPM
United States $2 - $8
Canada $2 - $7
United Kingdom $2 - $8
Australia $3 - $9
India $0.20 - $2

How Much Can a Gaming Channel Earn?

A gaming channel receiving 1 million views may earn anywhere from $1,000 to $8,000 depending on audience location and viewer demographics.

Many successful gaming creators earn more from sponsorships, memberships, affiliate programs and merchandise than from ad revenue alone.

Why Gaming CPM Is Lower

  • Large supply of gaming content
  • Younger audience demographics
  • Lower advertiser competition compared to finance
  • Higher dependence on sponsorship revenue
  • Strong global competition among creators

Frequently Asked Questions

Is gaming a good YouTube niche?

Yes. Gaming remains one of the biggest content categories on YouTube and can generate enormous view counts.

How much can 100,000 gaming views earn?

Typically between $100 and $800 depending on audience location and CPM rates.

Can gaming creators make money outside ads?

Absolutely. Sponsorships, memberships, donations and merchandise often generate substantial revenue.
