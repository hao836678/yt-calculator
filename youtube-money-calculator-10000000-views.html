How Much Does YouTube Pay for 10,000,000 Views?

Reaching 10 million YouTube views is a major milestone that can generate significant creator income.

Revenue depends on CPM, RPM, audience demographics, niche, viewer retention and advertiser demand.

Quick Answer

Most monetized YouTube channels earn between $10,000 and $300,000 for 10 million views.
