YouTube CPM Spain (2026)

Spain is one of the largest Spanish-speaking YouTube markets in Europe. Many creators target Spanish audiences because of strong advertiser demand and high engagement rates.

Spanish CPM rates are generally higher than most Latin American countries while remaining below the highest-paying markets such as the United States and Australia.

Average YouTube CPM in Spain

Metric Value
Average CPM $2 - $6
Finance CPM $8 - $20+
Business CPM $6 - $15+
Technology CPM $4 - $12+
Entertainment CPM $1 - $4

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in Spain?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from Spanish audiences can often earn between $2,000 and $6,000.

Finance, technology and business channels often earn significantly more than entertainment-focused content.

Why Is Spain CPM Higher Than Latin America?

  • Higher advertiser spending
  • Strong ecommerce market
  • Higher consumer purchasing power
  • Premium European advertisers
  • Competitive finance and technology sectors

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Spain good for YouTube monetization?

Yes. Spain offers higher CPM rates than many Spanish-speaking countries and remains one of Europe's strongest YouTube markets.

How much can 100,000 views earn in Spain?

Many creators earn between $200 and $600 depending on content niche and audience quality.

Which niche has the highest CPM in Spain?

Finance, investing, insurance, software and business channels generally receive the highest CPM rates.
