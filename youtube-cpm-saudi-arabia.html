YouTube CPM Saudi Arabia (2026)

Saudi Arabia is one of the highest-paying YouTube markets in the Middle East due to high consumer spending and strong advertiser competition.

Finance, luxury goods, ecommerce and real estate advertisers heavily target Saudi audiences.

Average YouTube CPM in Saudi Arabia

Metric Value
Average CPM$5 - $15
Finance CPM$18 - $45+
Business CPM$12 - $30+
Real Estate CPM$15 - $40+
Entertainment CPM$2 - $7

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in Saudi Arabia?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from Saudi audiences can often earn between $5,000 and $15,000.

Finance, luxury, real estate and business channels may earn substantially more.

Why Is Saudi Arabia CPM High?

  • High purchasing power
  • Luxury brand advertisers
  • Premium finance sector
  • Growing ecommerce market
  • Strong advertiser competition
