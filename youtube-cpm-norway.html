YouTube CPM Norway (2026)

Norway is one of the highest-income countries in the world and consistently delivers strong YouTube advertising rates. Norwegian audiences are highly valuable to advertisers because of their strong purchasing power.

Creators targeting Norwegian viewers often earn significantly higher CPM rates than the global average.

Average YouTube CPM in Norway

Metric Value
Average CPM $5 - $14
Finance CPM $15 - $35+
Business CPM $10 - $25+
Technology CPM $8 - $20+
Entertainment CPM $2 - $7

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in Norway?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from Norwegian audiences can often earn between $5,000 and $14,000.

Finance, investing, insurance and business channels frequently exceed these averages.

Why Is Norway CPM So High?

  • Very high purchasing power
  • Premium financial advertisers
  • Strong ecommerce market
  • High consumer spending
  • Competitive digital advertising industry

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Norway a high CPM country?

Yes. Norway consistently ranks among the highest CPM countries in Europe and globally.

How much can 100,000 views earn in Norway?

Many creators earn between $500 and $1,400 depending on niche and audience quality.

Which niche has the highest CPM in Norway?

Finance, insurance, investing, business and software content typically achieve the highest CPM rates.
