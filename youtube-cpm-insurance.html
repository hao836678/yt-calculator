YouTube CPM Insurance (2026)

Insurance is one of the highest CPM niches on YouTube. Insurance companies spend enormous budgets on customer acquisition because each new customer can generate revenue for many years.

As a result, advertisers are willing to pay premium CPM rates for insurance-related content.

Average Insurance Channel CPM

Audience Region Typical CPM
United States $15 - $60+
Canada $12 - $45+
United Kingdom $15 - $50+
Australia $15 - $55+
India $2 - $10

How Much Can an Insurance Channel Earn?

Insurance content often earns some of the highest advertising rates on YouTube. Even moderate traffic can generate substantial revenue.

Many creators supplement ad income with insurance affiliate programs and lead-generation partnerships.

Why Insurance CPM Is Extremely High

  • Very high customer lifetime value
  • Aggressive advertiser competition
  • Large insurance company marketing budgets
  • High-value lead generation market
  • Strong conversion potential

Frequently Asked Questions

Is insurance the highest CPM niche?

Insurance is consistently among the highest CPM niches online.

How much can 100,000 insurance views earn?

Depending on audience location and advertiser demand, earnings may reach several thousand dollars.

What insurance topics perform best?

Auto insurance, health insurance, life insurance and business insurance generally attract the highest advertiser bids.
