YouTube CPM Business (2026)

Business content is one of the most profitable categories on YouTube. Topics such as entrepreneurship, startups, investing, productivity and online business attract premium advertisers.

Business audiences often have higher purchasing power, making them extremely valuable for advertisers.

Average Business Channel CPM

Audience Region Typical CPM
United States $8 - $35+
Canada $7 - $30+
United Kingdom $8 - $32+
Australia $8 - $35+
India $1 - $8

How Much Can a Business Channel Earn?

Business creators often earn substantially more than average YouTubers because advertisers compete aggressively for entrepreneurial and professional audiences.

Channels covering startups, online business and productivity frequently generate strong ad revenue and sponsorship opportunities.

Why Business CPM Is High

  • High-income audience demographics
  • Premium B2B advertisers
  • Software and SaaS sponsorships
  • Strong affiliate marketing opportunities
  • High customer lifetime value

Frequently Asked Questions

Is business a high CPM niche?

Yes. Business is consistently among the highest CPM categories on YouTube.

How much can 100,000 business views earn?

Depending on audience quality and geography, earnings may range from hundreds to several thousand dollars.

What business topics perform best?

Entrepreneurship, online business, startups, productivity and marketing generally attract strong advertiser demand.
