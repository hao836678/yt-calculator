YouTube CPM New Zealand (2026)

New Zealand is a premium English-speaking advertising market with strong ecommerce activity and high-value advertisers.

Creators targeting New Zealand audiences often enjoy CPM rates well above the global average.

Average YouTube CPM in New Zealand

Metric Value
Average CPM$5 - $12
Finance CPM$15 - $35+
Business CPM$10 - $28+
Technology CPM$8 - $22+
Entertainment CPM$2 - $7

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in New Zealand?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from New Zealand audiences can often earn between $5,000 and $12,000.

Finance, investing, software and business channels often earn much more.

Why Is New Zealand CPM High?

  • High purchasing power
  • Premium English-speaking advertisers
  • Strong ecommerce market
  • Excellent ad conversion
  • Competitive digital advertising

Frequently Asked Questions

Is New Zealand a high CPM country?

Yes. New Zealand ranks among the highest CPM countries globally.

Many creators earn between $500 and $1,200 depending on niche.
