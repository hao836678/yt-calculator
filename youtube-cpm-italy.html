YouTube CPM Italy (2026)

Italy is one of Europe’s major digital advertising markets, with strong demand from finance, ecommerce, automotive and luxury brands.

Creators targeting Italian audiences generally earn CPM rates above the global average.

Average YouTube CPM in Italy

Metric Value
Average CPM$3 - $8
Finance CPM$10 - $24+
Business CPM$7 - $18+
Technology CPM$5 - $14+
Entertainment CPM$1.5 - $5

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in Italy?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from Italian audiences can often earn between $3,000 and $8,000.

Finance, luxury, SaaS and business channels often earn much more.

Why Is Italy CPM Moderate to High?

  • Large economy
  • Strong luxury brands
  • Growing digital ad spending
  • High ecommerce activity
  • Good advertiser competition
