YouTube CPM Tech (2026)

Technology is one of the highest-paying YouTube niches. Software companies, cloud platforms, AI tools and hardware brands invest heavily in digital advertising.

Tech creators often earn significantly higher CPMs than entertainment and gaming channels.

Average Technology Channel CPM

Audience Region Typical CPM
United States $6 - $20+
Canada $5 - $18+
United Kingdom $5 - $18+
Australia $6 - $20+
India $1 - $5

How Much Can a Technology Channel Earn?

A technology channel receiving 1 million views may earn between $3,000 and $20,000 depending on audience location, topic and advertiser demand.

AI, software reviews, SaaS tools, cybersecurity and cloud computing content generally command the highest CPMs.

Why Tech CPM Is High

  • Software companies have large advertising budgets
  • High-value business customers
  • Strong affiliate marketing opportunities
  • Rapidly growing AI industry
  • Premium advertiser competition

Frequently Asked Questions

Is technology a high CPM niche?

Yes. Technology consistently ranks among the highest CPM categories on YouTube.

How much can 100,000 tech views earn?

Many creators earn between $300 and $2,000 depending on audience quality and topic.

Which tech topics earn the most?

AI tools, SaaS software, cybersecurity, cloud services and business technology usually generate the highest CPM rates.
