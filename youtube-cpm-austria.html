YouTube CPM Austria (2026)

Austria is a strong Central European advertising market with premium audiences and stable advertiser demand.

Creators targeting Austrian viewers often receive CPM rates well above the global average.

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in Austria?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from Austrian audiences can often earn between $4,000 and $10,000.

Finance, insurance, business and software channels generally earn more.

Why Is Austria CPM High?

  • Strong economy
  • High purchasing power
  • Premium advertisers
  • Strong finance sector
  • High ad conversion rates
