YouTube CPM Taiwan (2026)

Taiwan is one of Asia’s strongest digital advertising markets, supported by high internet penetration and strong spending in technology and ecommerce.

Creators targeting Taiwanese audiences often earn CPM rates well above the global average.

Average YouTube CPM in Taiwan

Metric Value
Average CPM$4 - $12
Finance CPM$15 - $38+

Technology CPM$8 - $24+
Entertainment CPM$2 - $7

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in Taiwan?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from Taiwan audiences can often earn between $4,000 and $12,000.

Finance, technology and business channels often earn substantially more.

Why Is Taiwan CPM High?

  • Strong consumer spending
  • Major technology advertisers
  • Advanced ecommerce market
  • High purchasing power
  • Strong advertiser competition
