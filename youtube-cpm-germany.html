YouTube CPM Australia (2026)

Australia is consistently one of the highest CPM countries on YouTube. Australian advertisers spend aggressively on finance, insurance, software and e-commerce.

Channels targeting Australian audiences frequently achieve CPM rates that rival or exceed those in the United Kingdom and Canada.

Average YouTube CPM in Australia

Metric Value
Average CPM $5 - $13
Finance CPM $15 - $40+
Business CPM $10 - $30+
Technology CPM $8 - $20+
Entertainment CPM $3 - $8

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in Australia?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from Australian audiences may earn between $5,000 and $13,000.

Finance, investing, software and business channels often earn considerably more than entertainment content.

Why Is Australia CPM So High?

  • High consumer spending power
  • Premium English-speaking audience
  • Strong finance and insurance industries
  • High advertiser competition
  • Excellent conversion rates for advertisers

Frequently Asked Questions

Yes. Australia is among the highest CPM countries globally.

Many creators earn between $500 and $1,300 depending on niche and audience quality.

Is Australia CPM higher than Canada?

Often yes. Australia frequently ranks among the very highest CPM markets worldwide.
