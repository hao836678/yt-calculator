YouTube CPM Singapore (2026)

Singapore is one of the highest CPM countries in Asia. Advertisers spend heavily on finance, technology, software and luxury products.

Because of its strong economy and high purchasing power, Singaporean audiences are highly valuable to advertisers.

Average YouTube CPM in Singapore

Metric Value
Average CPM $5 - $15
Finance CPM $15 - $40+
Business CPM $10 - $30+
Technology CPM $8 - $25+
Entertainment CPM $3 - $8

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in Singapore?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from Singaporean audiences may earn between $5,000 and $15,000.

Finance, investing, SaaS and business content can often exceed these averages.

Why Is Singapore CPM So High?

  • High purchasing power
  • Premium finance sector
  • Strong technology industry
  • High advertiser competition
  • Excellent conversion rates

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Singapore a high CPM country?

Yes. Singapore is one of the highest CPM markets in Asia.

Many creators earn between $500 and $1,500 depending on niche and audience quality.

Yes. Singapore CPM rates are often many times higher than Indian CPM rates.
