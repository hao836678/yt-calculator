YouTube CPM USA (2026)

The United States remains one of the highest paying countries for YouTube creators. American advertisers spend significantly more than advertisers in most other regions.

As a result, channels targeting U.S. audiences often earn several times more revenue than channels targeting lower CPM countries.

Average YouTube CPM in the United States

Metric Value
Average CPM $4 - $12
Finance CPM $15 - $40+
Business CPM $10 - $30+
Technology CPM $8 - $25+
Entertainment CPM $2 - $8

How Much Does YouTube Pay for 1 Million Views in the USA?

A YouTube channel with 1 million views from United States audiences can often earn between $4,000 and $12,000.

Channels operating in finance, investing, software and business niches may earn significantly more.

Why Is USA CPM So High?

  • Large advertising budgets
  • High consumer purchasing power
  • Strong competition among advertisers
  • Premium finance and software advertisers
  • High conversion rates

Frequently Asked Questions

Is USA the highest CPM country?

The USA is consistently among the highest CPM countries, alongside Australia, Canada and the United Kingdom.

How much can 100,000 views earn in America?

Many creators earn between $400 and $1,200 depending on niche and audience quality.

Which niche has the highest CPM in the USA?

Finance, investing, software, insurance and business content typically achieve the highest CPM rates.
