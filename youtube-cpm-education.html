YouTube CPM Education (2026)

Educational content remains one of the most stable niches on YouTube. Tutorials, online learning, academic subjects and skill-based content attract long-term audiences.

Education channels often benefit from evergreen traffic and higher advertiser trust compared to entertainment content.

Average Education Channel CPM

Audience Region Typical CPM
United States $4 - $15
Canada $4 - $12
United Kingdom $4 - $14
Australia $5 - $15
India $0.50 - $4

How Much Can an Education Channel Earn?

Education channels often generate consistent revenue because their content remains relevant for years and continues attracting search traffic.

Many educational creators also earn additional income through courses, coaching and memberships.

Why Education CPM Is Strong

  • Evergreen search traffic
  • Trusted content category
  • Online course advertisers
  • Professional audience
  • Long watch times

Frequently Asked Questions

Is education a good YouTube niche?

Yes. Educational content can continue generating traffic and revenue for many years.

How much can 100,000 educational views earn?

Depending on audience location, earnings may range from several hundred to more than one thousand dollars.

What educational topics perform best?

Technology, business, language learning, career development and finance education generally attract higher CPM rates.
